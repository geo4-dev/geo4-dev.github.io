About Geo4Dev

Understanding our mission and partnership for geospatial development research.

Our Mission

The Geospatial Analysis for Development (Geo4Dev) Initiative promotes the use of geospatial data and methods in international development research and policy. The platform provides access to journals, datasets, tools, and training materials that help researchers leverage geographic information for evidence-based development interventions.

Geo4Dev is run in collaboration between New Light Technologies Inc., the Center for Global Action (CEGA) out of UC Berkeley, and the International Initiative for Impact Evaluation (3IE).

Key Objectives
  • Promote geospatial data in development research
  • Provide open access training materials
  • Curate relevant datasets and publications
  • Foster collaboration between researchers
  • Build capacity in geospatial analysis
