FAQ
How do I contribute a training module?
Contact us at info@geo4.dev with a proposal describing your module topic, target audience, and methodology. We welcome contributions from researchers and practitioners worldwide.
Is the platform free to use?
Yes! All Geo4Dev resources, training modules, and datasets are freely accessible. Our mission is to make geospatial data available to everyone.
How can I suggest a dataset?
Use the contact form above with the subject "Dataset Suggestion" to propose new datasets for inclusion in our library.
Are there upcoming conferences?
Check our Conference page for the latest schedule and register to receive notifications about future events.
